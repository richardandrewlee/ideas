# ideas

A place where things begin.

This is a journal of ideas — all of them. Half-formed thoughts, wild swings, quiet observations, things I'm afraid to say out loud, things I need to write down before they disappear. There's no filter here for viability or taste or timing. If it crossed my mind and felt worth keeping, it lives here.

Not a todo list. Not a product backlog. Not a portfolio.

Just the raw material.

---

Some of these will become something. Most won't. That's fine — the point isn't completion, it's honesty. Writing an idea down is an act of faith that it mattered enough to capture, even if I never come back to it.

If you're reading this and you're me: be kind to the version of yourself who wrote these. He was doing his best with what he knew at the time.

If you're reading this and you're not me: hi. I hope something here is useful or interesting or at least human.
